Frequently Asked Questions

how to register for clases etc.

You can register for classes through our Courses page. Browse available courses, select one that interests you, and follow the enrollment instructions. All our courses are free or low-cost!

Who are the cloud classroom students (global scope, etc., a lot of countries)

Cloud Classroom serves students from around the world. Our global community includes learners from Kenya, China, Australia, US, Canada, various European countries and many more who are eager to expand their knowledge and skills.

where are classes? online/in person

All Cloud Classroom courses are delivered online, making them accessible to students anywhere in the world. Classes are conducted virtually through video conferencing platforms.

Who are the target students (grade 2-9)

Our courses are primarily designed for K-12 students, with a focus on grades 2 through 9. We offer a range of subjects including math, English, science, computer science, and more to support young learners in their academic journey.
